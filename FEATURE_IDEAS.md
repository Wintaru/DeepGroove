# VinylCollector — Feature Ideas

## Your Ideas

### Wishlist
A separate tab for records you want to buy. Same search/Discogs flow as adding, but saves to a wish list instead of your collection. Could show estimated value, let you mark as "found" to move it to the collection.

### Share / Export Collection
Generate a static site (HTML/CSS) the user can push to GitHub Pages. Could also do simpler exports first: CSV, JSON, or a shareable PDF one-pager of the collection.

---

## Discovery & Organization

### Listening History / Play Log
Tap a record to log a "play." Track how often each record gets played, last played date, show "most played" in stats.

### Collection Value Tracking
Poll Discogs for current market prices periodically, chart how the collection's estimated value changes over time.

### Duplicate Detection
Warn when you try to add a record you already own (match on Discogs ID or artist + title).

### Shelves / Custom Lists
Organize records into named groups beyond genres: "Desert island discs," "Need to re-sleeve," "Loaned to friend."

---

## Social & Sharing

### Letterboxd-Style Reviews
Add a personal rating (1–5 stars) and review note per record. Separate from condition notes.

### "Now Playing" Widget
iOS home screen widget showing the last record you logged a play for.

### AirDrop / Nearby Share
Share a single record's details (or your whole collection card) with another VinylCollector user nearby.

---

## Utility

### Loan Tracker
Mark a record as loaned to someone (name + date). Badge or separate view for outstanding loans.

### Re-sleeve / Maintenance Reminders
Flag records that need cleaning or new inner sleeves. Simple checklist per record.

### Barcode Batch Scan
Rapid-fire scan mode: scan one barcode after another without going through the picker each time, review and confirm the whole batch at the end.
